Dreadtides: Official Trailer

Adobe After Effects, Adobe Premiere Pro, Unity

Copyright to HeadByte Studios
March 1, 2020

Game Released on April 2, 2020
Link to Steam Store Page

• Drafted the trailer plan and outline from scratch to tell a story. Using Unity's Timeline and Cinemachine features, I manually captured the displayed game footage for my later use in post-production.

• Built the project's main frame in Adobe Premiere Pro, organizing all of my video and audio assets accordingly to match my storyboarding.

• Communicated closely with the programmers and marketers of HeadByte Studios to bring my initial vision to fruition. Efficiently managed time and resources within our available technical and creative boundaries.

• Animated static illustrations in Adobe After Effects. Brought the portraits to life with the liquify, displacement map, and puppet tools to manipulate subtle movements in the scenery.

• Understanding the layers and depth of the pictures, I applied cage meshes over the backgrounds, characters, and scenery objects. This, in addition to the atmospheric particles, created a 3D parallax display upon the camera's movement.
